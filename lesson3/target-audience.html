Target-audience

This site is for Houstonians or anyone planning to visit the great city of Houston. The website will be available through a web browser on computers or phones.

Personas

  • Chris is your average teenager who likes to go play basketball outside
  • Adam is from Austin but visits Houston every weekend

Scenarios

  • Adam is not sure whether or not it is going to rain this weekend, he can check the site before leaving
  • Chris and his friends plan on playing basketball in downtown and wants to know how hot it'll be
  • Angie is going out with her friends at night and wants to know if she should wear jeans or a skirt
  • Christian is going to grill and wants to know what time the clouds will be out to shield him from the sun
  • Aime wants to take her kids to the park but doesn't know what time the sun will set
